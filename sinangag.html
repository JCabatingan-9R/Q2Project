Sinangag

Ingredients
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoon oil canola, vegetable of grape-seed
  • 8 cloves garlic minced or chopped
  • salt and pepper
Instructions
  1. Heat a large pan or wok over medium heat and add the cooking oil.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√® for about 2-3 minutes in a low heat until lightly golden brown. Remove the garlic with slotted spoon leaving the garlic-infused oil behind. Drain fried garlic with paper towel.
  3. In the same wok, return the heat to medium high heat add cooked rice and spread in the surface to evenly distributed. Stir for 3-5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Continue to cook until heated through.
  4. Return the fried garlic to the pan (save some for garnish, if desired). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Transfer to a serving plate and topped with crispy garlic. Serve and enjoy.
