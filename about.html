About Kobbong Eateries

Welcome to Kobbong Eateries, the best place to turn to when in need for Filipino food items! On this platform is a compilation of the most loved Filipino dishes by both foreigners and the Filipinos themselves.

At Kobbong Eateries, we believe that there is a deeper meaning to the food we Filipinos eat: these food items are filled with love passed on from one generation to another, and we should try our best acknowledging this love by eating these foods whole-heartedly.


What We Offer:

  • Popular Filipino Recipes to cook by yourself or with others
  • Organized and easy-to-learn instructions per recipe
  • A high percentage of success in the kitchen
  • A way to share your own recipe and have it shown on the site

Quick Guide:

  • Go to the Recipes category to find our loved and researched food items.
  • Go to the Submit Recipe section to share your own recipe.
  • Go to the Search Bar to look for certain recipes.


Whether you're a local or a foreigner, these Filipino food items are guaranteed to be tasty once tried. Join our community and learn more about the Filipino culture!
